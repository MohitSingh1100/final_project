Web Dev

Web development encompasses creating and maintaining websites and web applications, split into front-end and back-end development. Front-end development focuses on the visual aspects that users interact with, using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build and style the user interface. Back-end development, on the other hand, deals with server-side operations, including database management and application logic, utilizing languages like Python, Ruby, and PHP.

UX Design

UX (User Experience) design focuses on creating products that offer meaningful and efficient interactions for users. It involves researching user needs and behaviors, designing intuitive interfaces, and ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience. UX designers create wireframes and prototypes, plan interactions, and work on usability to make sure the product is easy to use and visually appealing.

Photo/Video

Photography skills involve understanding and applying various techniques to capture compelling and well-composed images. Key aspects include mastering camera settings such as aperture, shutter speed, and ISO to control exposure and focus, as well as learning about composition principles like the rule of thirds, leading lines, and framing. Good photographers also develop a keen eye for lighting and can adapt to different conditions, using natural or artificial light effectively.

Laser Engraving

Laser skills involve understanding and using laser technology effectively and safely. This includes knowledge of laser principles like wavelength and power, adhering to safety protocols to prevent hazards, and proficiently operating and maintaining laser equipment.
